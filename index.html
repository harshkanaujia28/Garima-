About me

Greetings! I'm Garima Verma, a graphic designer driven by a passion for creativity and an enduring love for drawing. My educational journey began at Kanpur Vidya Mandir Mahila Mahavidyalaya, where I cultivated my artistic abilities from high school through higher secondary education. Having earned a Bachelor of Arts degree from P.P.N. College, Kanpur University in 2023, I've since dedicated myself to merging my academic knowledge with practical experience in graphic design. From crafting impactful visuals to developing memorable brand identities, I'm committed to delivering designs that resonate with audiences. Drawing is not just a hobby but a cornerstone of my creative process, providing inspiration and fueling innovation in my design work. Through my portfolio, I aim to showcase the seamless fusion of artistry and technology, inviting viewers to join me on a journey where imagination knows no bounds. Let's collaborate and transform ideas into captivating visuals that leave a lasting impression.
